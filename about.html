My values & beliefs

Designing with heart and mind

Balance is key – not just in my everyday life but also in my work, where I focus on creating user-centered solutions. Solutions that are not only aesthetic or visually pleasing, but also functional and resonate on a human level, are essential to crafting experiences that are thoughtful, inclusive, and impactful.

Stay curious, keep growing

"Wonder is the beginning of wisdom" – Every question for me is a doorway to new knowledge, and each problem is an opportunity for growth. I'm constantly on a journey of exploration, user research, question-pondering, existence-questioning, and seeking solutions that simplify the complex and transform the ordinary into the extraordinary.

In someone else's shoes

Perspective-taking, for me, goes beyond skill – it's a guiding principle. I embrace empathy to understand others' viewpoints, a philosophy that is rooted in me since my multi-cultural childhood and one that drives my work now. This approach allows me to effectively identify user pain points and create solutions that balance user needs with business objectives.
