The Challenge

Online course development is currently a resource-heavy and time-consuming endeavor, demanding extensive curriculum planning, content creation, and technical engineering.

Question:

How can we create a platform that revolutionizes this process?

My Role(s)

Brand Manager

I made informed design decisions related to the brand and front-end components, including logo design, brand colors, typography, and iconography.

UX/UI Lead

I developed wireframes, mockups, interface prototypes, and assets using Adobe XD. I also oversaw the recruitment and communication for user research, integrating key findings and user pain points into successive iterations of the platform.

Executive Summary

Goals

Enable the rapid creation of comprehensive virtual courses that are significantly less time-consuming and costly. Dramatically streamline online education, making quality learning more accessible globally.

Solution

Introducing PAIGET, our innovative AI course generation platform. It empowers SMEs, educators, organizations, and learners to quickly and efficiently create courses on any topic in under 10 minutes, restructuring the approach to online course development.

User Research

Identifying Potential Users

SMEs (Subject Matter Experts) and Independent Educators

These are individuals with expertise in a particular subject area who are looking to create and share their knowledge through online courses. They value efficiency and ease of use, as they might not have extensive technical skills.

Educational
Institutions


Schools, universities, and online learning platforms seeking to expand their course offerings rapidly and efficiently. They need a tool that is scalable and can integrate with their existing systems.

Corporate Training Departments


These users are focused on developing training materials for employees in various sectors. They require a platform that is versatile enough to cater to different training needs and user levels.

User Persona

Insights from User Research

Research Questions

  1. How do educators and SMEs utilize existing online course creation tools?
  2. What are the primary challenges and pain points faced with current platforms?
  3. What features are most valued in an online course creation tool?
  4. How important is the speed of course creation?
  5. What level of technical expertise do educators possess, and how does this impact their use of digital tools?
  6. What are some accessibility issues that need to be considered?

Key Findings

    Educators, especially those with limited technical skills, prioritize ease of use and intuitive interfaces.

    There is a need for high levels of customization in course content and structure to cater to diverse educational needs.

    Demand for interactive and engaging tools that can elevate the virtual learning experience.

    A significant emphasis on reducing the time required to create comprehensive and quality courses.
